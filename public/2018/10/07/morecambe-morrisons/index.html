Morecambe Morrisons

2018/10/07

Morrisons Cafe, Morecambe

I went swimming with some ladies from MALLOWS (Morecambe and Lancaster Open Water Swimmers) in Morecambe Bay. The sea was calm although a little chilly. You can see the trace on Strava.

Afterwards I popped into Morrisons Morecambe to warm up with a breakfast. I had to wait 30 minutes, as they were super busy, but they did give me an extra coffee whilst I waited.
